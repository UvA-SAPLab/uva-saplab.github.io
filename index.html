Introduction

UvA-SAP Lab is a collaboration between SAP, ILLC and IvI and involves Ivan Titov and Max Welling as faculty as well as two PhD students (Thomas Kipf and Sergii Gavrylov) and a postdoc (Rianne van den Berg).

Research in SAP Lab focusses on the developing machine learning methods combined with natural language processing methods to analyse large networks. Applications range from knowledge graphs, citation networks, social networks and recommendation systems.
